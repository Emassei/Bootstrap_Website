About Me!

My name is Ernie Massei, born in Dallas Texas, and moved to Portland Oregon a year ago and I love it! I studied philosophy in Texas and thought that would be my life, but now I am looking toward the burgeoning tech community.

While not forgetting my liberal arts background, I have found a new passion in web development and content creation, specializing in HTML5, CSS frameworks, JavaScript, and CMSs.

Above all, aside from my new technical pursuits, I am fascinated with how technology affects our everyday lives and how we actively choose to live with this new facet of our existence!
